Style According to Your Body Structure

Hourglass (Female)

Balanced proportions, defined waist.

✅ Wrap dresses to enhance curves.
✅ High-waisted jeans to highlight the waist.
✅ Peplum tops for balance.
✅ Fitted blazers to define silhouette.

Pear (Female)

Wider hips, smaller upper body.

✅ A-line skirts to balance proportions.
✅ Off-shoulder tops to broaden shoulders.
✅ Dark-colored bottoms for slimming effect.
✅ Statement tops to draw attention upward.

Apple (Female)

Weight around midsection, slimmer legs.

✅ Empire waist dresses for elongation.
✅ V-neck tops to create length.
✅ Straight-leg pants for a balanced look.
✅ Flowy tops to avoid clinginess.

Rectangle (Female)

Equal bust, waist, and hip measurements.

✅ Belts to create waist definition.
✅ Layered outfits for depth.
✅ Ruffled tops to add volume.
✅ Flared skirts to create curves.

Inverted Triangle (Female)

Broad shoulders, narrower hips.

✅ A-line skirts to add volume to hips.
✅ V-neck tops to soften broad shoulders.
✅ Bootcut pants for balance.
✅ Simple, fitted tops with minimal details.

Triangle (Male)

Wider hips, narrow upper body.

✅ Structured blazers to add shoulder width.
✅ Dark trousers for a slimming effect.
✅ Vertical stripes for elongation.
✅ Fitted shirts to define the waist.

Inverted Triangle (Male)

Broad shoulders, narrow hips.

✅ Slim-fit trousers to balance proportions.
✅ Crew-neck shirts to soften upper body.
✅ Straight-cut jackets for symmetry.
✅ Avoid overly tight shirts.

Rectangle (Male)

Equal width shoulders, waist, and hips.

✅ Layered outfits for added depth.
✅ Blazers with structured shoulders.
✅ Patterns and textures for a dynamic look.

Find Your Face Shape & Perfect Makeup Tips

Oval Face

Balanced proportions with slightly wider cheekbones.

✅ Contour lightly to define features.
✅ Highlight cheekbones and brow bone.
✅ Try soft, curved eyebrows for a natural look.

Round Face

Equal width and height, soft curves.

✅ Contour the sides to create dimension.
✅ Apply blush slightly upwards for an elongated effect.
✅ Go for high-arched eyebrows to add structure.

Square Face

Strong jawline and broad forehead.

✅ Contour jawline to soften angles.
✅ Use round or soft eyeshadow blending.
✅ Opt for curved, slightly arched eyebrows.

Heart Face

Wider forehead and pointed chin.

✅ Focus contour on forehead and chin.
✅ Highlight the center of the face for balance.
✅ Soft, rounded eyebrows work best.

Diamond Face

Narrow forehead and jaw with prominent cheekbones.

✅ Highlight cheekbones for a glowing effect.
✅ Contour the forehead and jaw to soften edges.
✅ Try a curved, well-defined eyebrow shape.

Oblong Face

Elongated shape with balanced proportions.

✅ Apply blush on the apples of cheeks to add width.
✅ Avoid excessive contouring to keep the face balanced.
✅ Straight or softly curved eyebrows work best.
